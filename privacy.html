Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

We operate the website cozygrowgarden.com and the Garden Planner application at mygardenplanner.app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

What We Collect

  • Account data: If you create an account, we store your email address and authentication credentials securely via Supabase.
  • Garden data: Garden layouts, plant selections, and settings you save are stored in your account.
  • Location data: If you use the weather feature, your approximate location is stored locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-friendly analytics. No personal data is shared with third parties for advertising.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Garden Planner service. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Stripe: Payment processing — we never see or store your credit card details
  • Vercel: Website hosting
  • Open-Meteo: Weather data (only coordinates sent, no personal data)

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app. Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out via the Garden Planner app at mygardenplanner.app.
